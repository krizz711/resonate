The aim

Billions type their most honest words into an AI — not into a Bible app.

Grief at 2 a.m. Exam panic. “I feel like I’m failing everyone.” Those sentences are poured into chatbots every day — not to a pastor, not to a Bible. Scripture has never been present in that room. Not because it doesn’t belong there — because no one built the bridge.

Resonate is the quiet bridge: verified Scripture woven into the conversation you’re already in — safely, and only when it truly fits.

IVSecurity & safety

When it hears darkness, it sends people — not verses.

Crisis text is caught on the raw input and routed to a human-help card, never a verse — 100% recall on the eval set. Restraint and rate-limiting keep it quiet; privacy by design keeps it local. It reads only your own message and stores nothing.
