Give your AI assistant
a sense of Scripture.

Resonate runs as an MCP server — a small tool your assistant can call. Add it once, and Claude, Gemini, ChatGPT, Copilot, Cursor, Antigravity, Windsurf — any app that speaks MCP — can reach for a verified verse, weave a story, or pull reels, mid-conversation. You don't change your chat app; you hand it a new sense.

1Your Resonate Key

One key is you. Use the same key in ChatGPT, in Claude, in Cursor, on your phone and your laptop — and they all share one context: the same recurring themes, the same “you've returned to this lately”. Different AIs, one individual. Save this key; carrying it to a new device carries your whole history.

2Add by URL — nothing to install

The fastest path, and it works from any device: your assistant connects to the hosted Resonate server over the internet. Your key is already baked into this URL — copy it, then follow your app below.

3Or run it on your machine

Prefer everything local — offline, nothing over the network? Clone the repo (github.com/krizz711/resonate, Python 3.11+) and point your app at the file. The --key ties this local copy to the same brain as everything above.

4Grok, or any chat that fetches URLs

Some chats that can't host a tool can still fetch a URL you give them (Grok, DeepSeek and similar). Paste this once at the top of a conversation — it teaches the chat to fetch your Resonate line when you share a feeling. ChatGPT is not one of them: its browsing searches the web but won't call an API, so on ChatGPT use the extension (step 2's ChatGPT tab) — that's its zero-setup path.
…
Heads-up on this path: whether the chat actually fetches is the model's choice each turn — if it answers without fetching, say "fetch that URL and quote what it returns." A browsing chat also sends your words as part of a web address, so this is less private than the MCP URL above (which sends them in a request body). Use it for reach; use Add by URL or the extension when it matters.

5What your assistant can now do

Four tools, the same guarantees as everywhere else — safety first, verses verbatim, nothing stored.
resonate_verse

A moment → one verified verse, or an honest silence. Crisis → help, never a verse.

generate_story

Weaves the moment into a vetted biblical narrative, labeled — ending on a verbatim verse.

reel_groups

Prioritized sets of story reels for the moment and the threads you return to.

fetch_passage

Verbatim licensed passage text by reference, straight from YouVersion.

Why a tool, not a chatbot? Because a billion people already pour their real lives into an AI — not a Bible app. Resonate meets them there: it teaches the assistant they already trust to reach for Scripture at the right moment, with the words verified and a human-help card when it hears darkness. That's the whole idea — Scripture, where you already are.
