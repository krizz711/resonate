Security · guardian alerts

People who should know
if you're not okay.

If Resonate ever hears a moment of real crisis in what you write, it can quietly reach the people you trust — by WhatsApp or email. They're only told to check on you; what you wrote is never shared. This is yours to set, and yours to turn off.

How it works. Crisis text is caught on your raw input before anything else and answered with human help lines — never a verse. Only then, and only if you've consented above, your guardians are notified once (with a 24-hour cool-down so a hard night never becomes a flood). Everything is stored locally on this engine. Leaving all fields empty and saving removes your registration entirely.
